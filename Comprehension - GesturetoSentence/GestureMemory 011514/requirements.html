System Requirements

This experiment requires a browser that can display videos. Common modern browsers including the current versions of Firefox, Safari, and Chrome should all work well.

Next you will watch a test video to make sure your system will display them correctly. When you are ready, click the button below to watch the test video. You will be asked to write a short description of what you see. If your browers hangs up or the video does not play all the way to the end, you should not take this survey.
