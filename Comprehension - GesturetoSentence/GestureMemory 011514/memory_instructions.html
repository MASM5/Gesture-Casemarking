Memory Task Instructions

Now that you have learned a vocabulary of gestures, you will watch a series of 72 memory sets. In each set, you will be given lists of numbers to remember, and a movie showing a gesture "phrase." These phrases do not necessarily use English grammar, but the agent (the person doing the action) will always come at the beginning of the phrase.



Your task will be to report the lists of numbers you see and at the same time, remember what the gesture phrase means.



The movies are very short, and the presentations are timed. Do not click away from this tab during the experiment. If you are looking away, or if a movie doesn't display correctly, click the "I missed it" button.
